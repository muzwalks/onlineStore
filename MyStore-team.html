THE TEAM


Jonas Whitman - Founder

Jonas worked in the China film industry for 10 years. While there he built solid relationships with equipment manufactures which allowed him to later found Apache. It is because of these relationships that Apache cameras, lighting and sound are of superior quality offered at an affordable rental price. He is also currently facilitating film co-productions between South Africa and China. Murray speaks fluent Chinese which makes him an integral player in the co-production space.

Darrell Jenkins - Founder

Darrell is regarded as South Africa's most prolific film director with 47 credits to date. He is probably most well known for his 1992 film, Sarafina, which starred actress Whoopi Goldberg. He co-founded Apache Rentals with Jonas Whitman back in 2010. As a directer himself, he knows how important it is to your film budget to operate reliable and top-notch equipment. He is also available for hire on any productions who face directing challenges or for those inexperienced crews needing help with our camera equipment.

Victor Hugo - Head of China Operations

Victor spends his time between Beijing and Johannesburg. Born and raised in Shanghai, he knows the Chinese market very well and is constantly visiting the Guangzhou manufacturers sourcing out new suppliers who can provide even better cost-quality ratioed products. He has spent the last 25 years in the export business and knows the supply chain intimately. Murray and Victor met on the production of Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon and have been friends and partners ever since.
